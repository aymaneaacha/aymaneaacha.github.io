Overview

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) was performed on a dataset containing 92,124 anonymized bicycle trip records from the Los Angeles Metro Bike Share program. The goal was to clean, validate, and prepare the dataset for future modeling or deeper analysis.

Key Insights

  • Dataset had 4 columns with missing values (coordinates); 5.8% of rows were dropped.
  • No duplicate records found.
  • Data types were corrected for start_time, end_time, and bike_id.
  • Outliers in trip_duration (up to 24 hours) were retained pending domain validation.
  • 46 records with Testing as a passholder type were removed as invalid.
  • Label encoding was applied to trip_route_category, passholder_type, and bike_type.

Conclusion

The dataset was successfully cleaned, structured, and is now ready for predictive modeling or time-series trend analysis. Key steps included resolving datatypes, handling missing values, and removing irrelevant or incorrect data entries.
